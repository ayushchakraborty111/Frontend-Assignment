    The two axes of flexbox


    While working with flexbox you need to think in terms of two axes, the main axis and the cross axis.


    The main axis is defined by the flex-direction property, and the cross axis run perpendicular to it. Everything we do with

    FlexBox refers to these axes, so it is worth understanding how they work from offset.
